✨ About the Poet

"Black Broken Heart" is a collection of poetic expressions exploring themes of love, loss, and personal transformation. Each poem is carefully crafted to evoke deep emotions and introspection through the beauty of language.

Hailing from Malawi, the poet draws inspiration from African traditions, personal experiences, and the universal human condition. Writing in both English and Chichewa, the poetry bridges cultures and speaks to hearts across the world.

"In every tear, there lies a hidden smile waiting to bloom."

— Black Broken Heart
